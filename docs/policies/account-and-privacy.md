# Acme Corp Account and Privacy Policy

## Account Creation

Customers must provide a valid email address and create a password to register an Acme Corp account. Accounts are limited to one per person. Customers must be at least 18 years old to create an account.

## Account Security

Customers are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials. Acme Corp uses industry-standard encryption to protect account data. Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available and recommended for all accounts. If a customer suspects unauthorized access, they should reset their password immediately and contact support.

## Data Collection

Acme Corp collects the following personal information: name, email address, shipping address, billing address, phone number, and payment information. This data is used solely for order processing, customer support, and improving our services.

## Data Sharing

Acme Corp does not sell customer data to third parties. Data may be shared with shipping carriers for delivery purposes and with payment processors for transaction processing. Acme Corp may disclose data when required by law or to protect its legal rights.

## Data Deletion

Customers may request deletion of their account and associated personal data by contacting support@acmecorp.com. Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Certain data may be retained as required by law (tax records, fraud prevention).

## Cookies

The Acme Corp website uses cookies for session management, analytics, and personalization. Customers can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit certain website functionality.
