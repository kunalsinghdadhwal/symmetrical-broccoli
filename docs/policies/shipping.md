# Acme Corp Shipping Policy

## Domestic Shipping

Acme Corp offers three domestic shipping options within the United States:

- Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days, free on orders over $50. Orders under $50 incur a flat $5.99 shipping fee.
- Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days, $12.99 flat rate.
- Overnight Shipping: Next business day delivery, $24.99 flat rate. Orders must be placed before 2:00 PM EST for same-day processing.

## International Shipping

Acme Corp ships to Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, France, Australia, and Japan. International shipping takes 10-15 business days. Rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the customer.

## Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed on the next business day. Customers receive a tracking number via email once the order has shipped.

## Delivery Issues

If a package is lost or not delivered within the expected timeframe, customers should contact support. Acme Corp will initiate a carrier investigation. If the package cannot be located within 10 business days of the investigation, a full refund or reshipment will be provided.

## PO Box and APO/FPO Addresses

Acme Corp ships to PO Box and APO/FPO addresses via USPS only. Expedited and overnight shipping options are not available for these addresses.
